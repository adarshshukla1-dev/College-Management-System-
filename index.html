About Our University

Learn about our history, mission, and values

Our History

Founded in 1952, our university has grown from a small college into a leading institution of higher learning. Over the decades, we've maintained our commitment to academic excellence while adapting to the changing needs of society.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide transformative education that empowers students to become leaders in their fields and contribute meaningfully to society. We envision a world where knowledge and innovation drive positive change.

Core Values

  • Academic Excellence
  • Innovation and Research
  • Diversity and Inclusion
  • Community Engagement
  • Sustainability

Admission Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • SAT/ACT scores (optional)
  • Personal statement
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Portfolio (for art programs)
